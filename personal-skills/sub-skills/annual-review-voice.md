# Annual Review Voice

How Nicole writes performance/review content.

## Framing

- **Concrete accomplishments tied to business outcomes.** "Replaced 7 SQL rule queries with one ML model running nightly with zero manual intervention" beats "Improved seller reporting."
- **Metrics over adjectives** where possible.
- **Growth mindset for weaknesses** — not self-criticism theater. Frame gaps as forward-looking investments, not failures.
- **Specific, named contributions.** Skills built, MCP servers shipped, PRs reviewed at scale, hackathon projects.

## Honest Gaps to Acknowledge

- Follow-through on exploratory projects. Starts a lot, doesn't always finish — fix is scope/success-criteria upfront.
- PR review cadence is wave-shaped — fix is consistency over heroics.
- Wants more backend depth, more formal mentorship, more visible technical leadership.

## Career Direction

- **Current:** Transitioning from Software Engineering into AI Solutions Engineering.
- Annual review and growth content should reflect this pivot — frame accomplishments in terms of AI product impact, customer/business outcomes, and cross-functional influence, not just IC engineering delivery.

## Anti-Patterns

- ❌ Adjective-heavy self-praise ("crushed it," "exceeded expectations").
- ❌ Burying accomplishments in modest framing.
- ❌ Listing tasks instead of outcomes.
- ❌ Pretending there are no gaps.
- ❌ Listing gaps without forward-looking actions.
